CWALM Corpus: Analyzing the Moroccan Dialect on Facebook

The CWALM Project utilizes a corpus collected from various social media platforms, particularly Facebook, to analyze and understand the Moroccan dialect. This corpus focuses on interactions and posts in Moroccan Arabic, providing insights into the linguistic structures used by native speakers. Below, you will find five stories analyzed from Facebook posts and comments, showcasing the richness and diversity of the Moroccan dialect.
