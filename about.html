About CWALM Project

Our Mission

The CWALM (Contemporary Written Arabic Lexical Model) Project aims to provide a comprehensive and validated resource for the analysis of Modern Standard Arabic and its dialects. Our project is designed to support linguistic research and applications that require high-quality corpus data, offering a new approach to understanding contemporary written Arabic through objective and balanced corpus data.

Our Vision

We envision a future where CWALM will become a valuable resource for researchers, educators, and developers working in the fields of computational linguistics, artificial intelligence, and natural language processing. By creating a unified and detailed corpus that accounts for various dialects, our goal is to improve the understanding of written Arabic and enhance the development of AI models capable of processing the language in all its forms.
